About

GratiToad is here to help you manage anxiety by building a simple daily habit: practicing gratitude.

When you write down things you’re thankful for, you feed your GratiToad — a playful way to encourage positive thinking and shift your focus away from stress.

The more gratitude you share, the stronger and happier your toad becomes, giving you a visual reminder of your progress.

At the same time, you can write down your anxious or negative thoughts as “anxiety bugs” — little symbols of what’s troubling you.

Your GratiToad will eat these bugs when you’ve fed it enough gratitude, helping you feel relief and regain control over overwhelming feelings.

GratiToad is designed to:

  • Encourage daily mindfulness through quick, easy gratitude entries
  • Help you recognize and confront anxious thoughts by writing them down
  • Provide visual motivation as your toad grows and defeats anxiety bugs
  • Support your mental well-being with reminders, affirmations, and progress tracking
  • Make managing anxiety less intimidating by turning it into a friendly, interactive experience

Whether you want to reduce stress, practice gratitude, or just need a gentle way to check in with yourself — GratiToad is your supportive companion every step of the way.
